ABOUT MUNCHBOX

Our mission is to provide healthy, wholesome packed lunches made with:

✓ No Preservatives

Preservatives, such as sulfites, can trigger wheezing and other asthma symptoms

✓ No saturated fats

Saturated fats increase your LDL (bad) cholesterol which can deposit in your arteries

✓ No unwanted salts or sugars

Salt puts a lot of pressure on children's kidneys and a lot of the sugar we eat is hidden in the food we buy – even in savoury food

✓ Local and ethically sourced produce

Supporting our local farmers and reducing our carbon footprint
